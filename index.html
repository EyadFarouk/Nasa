Today's Space News

Perseverance Rover Discovers Ancient River Delta

NASA's Perseverance rover has found evidence of an ancient river delta in Jezero Crater, suggesting the area was once filled with water and potentially habitable for microbial life.

Webb Telescope Captures Stunning Galaxy Images

The James Webb Space Telescope has captured unprecedented images of distant galaxies, revealing new details about the early universe and star formation processes.

Space Station Crew Conducts Spacewalk

Astronauts aboard the International Space Station successfully completed a spacewalk to install new solar arrays, extending the station's power capabilities for future missions.

Artemis Program Prepares for Lunar Return

NASA's Artemis program continues preparations for returning humans to the Moon, with new developments in lunar landing technology and astronaut training programs.
